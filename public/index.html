21 NOV. 16

CEFC Senior Care Facility Launch Ceremony & Medical Cluster Conference

It was a celebratory day in Shenzhen on November 21, 2016. The China EU Future City held its first annual Medical Cluster Conference and held a ceremony on launching the CEFC Senior Care Facility.

Both events held in the morning were joined by a delegation of German government officials from the Mayor of Erlangen City, Dr. Florian Javik, who presented at the conference and was in Shenzhen to promote greater ties between the two cities. The Mayor was also joined by Matthias Hegel, a Managing Director from the Medical Valley Center and Dr. Friedrich von Tucher, partner with Euro Sino Invest – Technology Expansion Partners.

As China’s population begins to age more rapidly, elderly care and living are going to become a higher priority in Chinese society. In this spirit, the conference saw presentations on the role European Medical Clusters and Dual Education will play in the CEFC Senior Care Facility and the impact it will have on China – Europe cooperation in these areas. The Senior Care Facility will have a vocational training campus nearby, thereby creating new opportunities for students to learn on the job on best practices and training to assist in caring for elderly Chinese.

17 JUNE 16

Official Ground Breaking Ceremony

4th Shenzhen International Low Carbon City and CEFC Groundbreaking Ceremony

On June 17th, the fourth Shenzhen International Low Carbon City Forum was held in Longgang District, with around 2000 guests from over 40 countries and international institutions attending. This year’s Forum centered in on the theme of Green Innovation: New Momentum for Urban Transformation and Development.

ESI was a major participant at the Forum, participating in the signing of MoUs and inviting Chinese and European officials to the official ground-breaking ceremony of the China EU Future City project. After many years of planning, the construction of the first phase of the project began.

The Forum played host to many keynote speakers. Both the Deputy Director of NDRC Zhang Yong, Mayor of Shenzhen XU Qing gave opening speeches. Audiences heard from other speakers such as former US Vice-President, and founder and chairman of Climate Reality Project, Al Gore, and former UN Deputy Secretary-General Sha Zukang, Director of the National Committee of Experts on Climate Change, former vice president of Chinese Academy of Engineering, academician Du Xiangwan, counselor of the State Council, former Vice Minister of Ministry of Science and Technology Liu Yanhua, Counselor of the State Council, former Vice Minister of Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development Qiu Baoxing, and vice mayor of Paris Patrick Krugman. Each delivered key-note speeches on topics regarding green innovation, and balanced urban development.

Some well-known international institutions were also included for the co-sponsorship of this forum. High-level officials and experts from more than 40 international institutions and countries, including United Nations, the World Bank, the United States, Canada, Britain, Sweden, the Netherlands, South Africa, France, and Greece attended the forum.
