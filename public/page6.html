CEFC ISN’T JUST A PROJECT, IT IS ALSO A PARTNERSHIP BETWEEN MANY STAKEHOLDERS
IN CHINA AND EUROPE WHO WANT TO ACHIEVE A MORE SUSTAINABLE FUTURE.

It is a pilot project under the EU-China Partnership on Urbanization launched in 2012 by Chinese and EU leaders.

Thanks to the support of Shenzhen Mayor Xu Qin, the CEFC Project is realized in Longgang District of Shenzhen, with the ground breaking ceremony taking place in June 17th, 2016. The project developer, Huan De Group, which is the development brand of Tucher & Schmied Family office for Chinese projects, will work closely with its European partners in relevant sectors to develop this project into a sustainable city as well as a regional commercial hub, combining industrial, production, commercial and residential areas based on eco-friendly design.

The stake holders

The project magnitude and the fact of CEFC being a pilot project initiated by the EU-China Partnership on Urbanisation explain the engagement of national and local governments and organizations from both the European and Chinese side. CEFC wishes to involve all the following stakeholders into the process aiming at supporting the project and holding regular discussions to facilitate the achievement of project goals.
