  • Huan De City is the first phase to be developed in the CEFC project. It is the symbol of the concept and technologies used in the CEFC project, and it’ll provide facilities for research, seminar, training, services, and primary entry of European companies.

  • The business and production center occupies an area of 1.036.098 qm, designed to attract and boost the European and Chinese 4.0 industries, with a special focus on IT & Smart Industry 4.0, New Material, High end Equipment & Manufacturing.

  • The Life Science Park is extended by 1.030.086 sqm. It is designed to represent the 1st Medical Valley in China, equipped with Incubator, Hospital, Medical campus, R&D, Senior Care Vocational Center, Senior Care Centre and all kinds of organization in medical field, which can provide effective and efficient healthcare solutions.

  • The Sustainable Urban Park is a system that balances and maximizes multiple social and environmental goals. It focused on supporting public access to non-polluting vehicles and pedestrian accessibility to the urban spaces.

Innovative features

CEFC is the new integration model for Shenzhen International Low Carbon City: a transformation of the existing Liulian community into a sustainable city as well as a regional commercial hub, combining industrial, production, commercial and residential areas based on eco-friendly design.
